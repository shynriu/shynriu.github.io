Oneiroi is a VR school project made in C# and Unity with friends in South Korea.

Blade Fruits and guns

BFG is a VR school project made in C# and Unity with friends in South Korea.

This game takes inspiration from many games such as Beat Saber and Fruit Ninja. Your goal is to slice fruits with your left hand holding a sword, and shoot to bombs using your gun in right hand
